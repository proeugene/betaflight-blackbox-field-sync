How it works

01

FC flash is full. Land.

02

Plug FC into the Pi.

03

LED confirms. Unplug.

04

Fly again. Repeat.

Why

Before: laptop
or dongle.

FC flash fills up fast. Clearing it used to mean a laptop and Configurator, or an expensive third-party dongle — either way, you're done flying.

Now: a Pi Zero W in your bag. Plug in, wait 30 seconds, fly again. Logs are timestamped and ready to analyse at home.

You need

Pi Zero W / Zero 2 W microSD Battery bank OTG cable Betaflight SPI flash FC
⚠
SPI flash only. Works with Betaflight internal SPI flash. Not FC SD-card blackbox.
